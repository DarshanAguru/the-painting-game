Welcome {{name}}, Before you start, let me give you some information about the game and scoring. There are a total of 10 doors in the palace and a room where the painting is kept, out of which 2 are set up as traps and lead to dead ends from which there's no way out. Each question will carry a score of 20, and the score will be reduced by (divided by) the number of minutes you spend solving that question (please leave no blank spaces in the answers).
